How it works

01

Choose your access

Subscribe for $6/month to stream via the Public Art Collections app on your smart TV — or buy a USB drive for $39 for a one-time offline experience. Both work on any modern TV.

02

Public Art Collections plays on your TV

Your TV cycles through 500,000+ museum masterpieces. The display rotates every 30–90 seconds in a mix of styles — sometimes minimal and full-bleed, sometimes with an ornate frame border, sometimes with a museum-style caption at the bottom.

03

Scan the QR code on screen

Every artwork shows a small QR code in the corner. Scan it with your phone to instantly open that work's page — read the full artist biography, historical context, and order a print or product in seconds.

04

Use your remote for info

Press any button on your TV remote while a work is displayed to trigger a popup showing the title, artist, movement, and year — right on the TV screen, no phone needed.

05

Order prints from your sofa

When you've scanned a QR code on your phone, you're one tap from ordering that work as a fine art print, canvas wrap, mug, tee, or phone case — fulfilled by Printful and delivered to your door.

06

Curated collections

Subscribers get new curated themed collections weekly — a collection of dawn landscapes, a week of Baroque portraits, a month of works by women. Or set your own filters to only show art that matches your room's style.
